We use the collected information to improve our app, provide personalized services, send you notifications, and conduct data analysis.

IV. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We may need to share your information in legal requirements or to respond to legal procedures.

V. Information Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, modification, or destruction. VI. User Rights

You have the right to request us to access, correct, delete your personal information, or request us to stop processing your personal information. If you have such a request, please contact us through our support channels.

We may update this Privacy Policy based on business needs or legal changes. When the policy changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications or other means. Please check this policy regularly to understand any changes.
